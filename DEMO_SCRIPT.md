# MOSAIC — 2-Minute Demo Script
**For investor meetings, demo days, and VC calls**

---

## Opening Frame (15 seconds)
*Before touching the screen:*

> "I'm going to show you what it looks like when a gig worker earns $14.50 on their way to work — and what a CPG brand sees on the other side in real time. Two minutes. No slides."

*Open MOSAIC_DEMO.html · dismiss the intro · start in Collector mode.*

---

## COLLECTOR JOURNEY (60 seconds)

### C1 — Onboarding
> "Maya is a commuter in London. She's heard Mosaic pays £12–£22 for tasks that take 8 minutes — on her route. She doesn't change her commute. She earns on the commute she's already taking."

*Point to the Commute Mode feature card.*

> "This is the key insight competitors missed: people already go past stores every day. We just built the infrastructure to pay them for it."

### C2 — Task Feed
> "Mosaic has matched four tasks to Maya's exact route today. The top one — a shelf audit at Sainsbury's Borough Market, 4 minutes north — pays $14.50 for 8 minutes of work."

*Pause for a beat.*

> "That's $109/hr equivalent. The worker sees exactly what to do, where to go, and how much she earns before she accepts."

### C3 — Map View (skip quickly, 5 seconds)
> "Route-matched tasks visible on a live map. She's already walking this way."

### C4 — Task Detail (10 seconds)
> "Step-by-step instructions. She walks to Aisle 6 and photographs the shelf."

### C5 — Camera (5 seconds)
> "On-device AI finds the shelf automatically. One tap to capture."

### C6 — AI Verify
> "This is where the magic is. The photo goes through our extraction pipeline — watch the AI scan."

*Wait for the scanning animation to complete.*

> "In under 30 seconds: 4/4 facings compliant, stock at 62%, eye-level placement confirmed. 92/100. Structured JSON generated automatically."

### C7 — Payout
> "She earns $14.50 instantly. Base pay plus a quality bonus because her score was 92. Transferred to her bank before she reaches the office."

*Let the confetti animation play.*

> "We built this experience to feel better than Uber. Because if the supply side loves you, the supply side compounds."

---

## ENTERPRISE JOURNEY (45 seconds)

*Switch to Enterprise mode. Move to Dashboard.*

> "Now switch to the other side. Same data, 4 hours later. This is what Meridian Foods — the brand that posted the task — sees."

### E1 — Dashboard
> "247 stores audited. 87.4% compliance score. 12 critical issues. Click on any metric."

*Click a metric card to show the drill-down tooltip.*

> "Every number is drillable. Not a static report — a live intelligence feed."

### E2 — Campaign Creator (10 seconds)
> "Posting a new campaign takes 90 seconds. Change the store count — the cost estimate updates in real time."

*Change the store count input.*

> "No sales call. No account manager. Self-serve, like Stripe."

### E4 — Gallery (10 seconds)
> "Every submitted photo with AI compliance overlay. You can search by store. Click any image."

*Type a store name in the search bar. Open one gallery item.*

> "Actual photo evidence, AI-extracted data, collector verified. Audit trail your compliance team will love."

### E5 — Compliance Report (5 seconds)
> "Exportable PDF report in one click. Trend arrows show week-on-week movement."

### E6 — Alerts (5 seconds)
> "Proactive AI alerts. Co-op Shoreditch is out of stock. One click to trigger a replenishment order."

---

## Close (10 seconds)
> "That's the full loop — from a commuter's morning to a brand's live compliance dashboard — in under 4 hours, for $8–25 per store. Versus $50–300 and 3 weeks with anyone else."

> "The product is built. The unit economics work. We're raising $5M to prove three cities. Want to see the financials?"

## Handling Interruptions

**"Where's the revenue?"** → "Pre-launch. We built the product first. First pilots this month."

**"Why won't BeMyEye copy this?"** → "They built for enterprise contracts over 15 years. They can't retrofit a consumer tier or on-device GDPR. We saw the same thing with Airbnb vs hotels."

**"What's the take rate?"** → "40% fixed payout to agents, 60% platform. Blended GM: 55% early, 65%+ at scale."
