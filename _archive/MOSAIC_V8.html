App Design Principles

01
Commute Mode

Tasks are filtered to within 0.5mi of the collector's current route. No detours required. The map shows only relevant tasks — not a sea of pins.

02
Task Card

Task type, payout, distance, SLA, and a single-tap claim. Designed for under 3 seconds to claim while on a moving bus or train.

03
Capture Flow

Guided in-store capture. Overlay shows which photos are needed. On-device AI validates in real-time — no blurs, obstructions, or GPS drift accepted.

04
Proof Lock

GPS coordinates, timestamp, and device ID embedded in every photo. Cryptographically signed proof — collector cannot submit photos taken elsewhere.

05
Gamification

Tier system (Bronze → Silver → Gold → Diamond). Streak bonuses. City leaderboard. Power collectors earn 25–40% more through priority task access and tier bonuses.

06
Instant Payout

Payouts to Wise, PayPal, or local bank within 24 hours of task approval. No minimum balance required to withdraw. Local currency in 40+ countries.

The Intelligence Layer

AI Extraction Pipeline — Photo to JSON in Minutes

Five stages from raw photo to structured, confidence-scored output. Designed for enterprise-grade accuracy with consumer-grade speed.

Stage 1: On-Device Validation

Before upload, the mobile app runs real-time validation on each photo:

  • Blur detection — rejects photos below sharpness threshold
  • Coverage check — ensures full shelf section is visible
  • GPS lock — verifies device is within 30m of target store
  • Timestamp bind — records exact capture time, prevents pre-taken photos

Stage 2: Cloud Extraction

GPT-4o-mini (launch) → fine-tuned Llama 70B (scale). Extracts:

  • SKU presence — is the specified product on shelf? (Y/N + confidence)
  • Shelf position — facing count, shelf level, adjacency
  • Price tag — current retail price, promotional tags present
  • Out-of-stock flag — empty facing, out-of-stock label, ghost facing
  • Planogram compliance — actual vs. agreed placement (0–100 score)

Stage 3: Quality Scoring

Every extraction receives a confidence score. Low-confidence extractions routed to human review:

  • Confidence >92% — auto-approved, delivered to client immediately
  • Confidence 75–92% — human review within 30 minutes
  • Confidence <75% — task re-assigned to a second collector for verification
  • Dispute flag — client can challenge any result within 48 hours

Product Roadmap

What Gets Built, When, and Why

18-month build plan tied to revenue milestones. AI-native team of 2 at launch, scaling to 8 engineers by Series A.

Q2–Q3 2026 (Pre-Launch)

  • Collector app MVP — iOS + Android, core capture flow, GPS validation, payout
  • Enterprise portal MVP — task creation, map view, results delivery
  • AI extraction v1 — GPT-4o-mini, shelf compliance + OOS + price
  • Payment infrastructure — Stripe + Wise integration, 40+ countries
  • Quality scoring v1 — basic confidence scoring, human review queue

Q4 2026–Q2 2027 (Scale)

  • API v1 — public REST API, webhooks, Salesforce integration
  • Fine-tuned AI v2 — Mosaic-specific model on 500K+ labeled images
  • Shopify app — SMB self-serve onboarding for DTC brands
  • Dispute resolution — automated distributor dispute package generation
  • Insurance vertical MVP — property condition task type, insurance-grade proofs
The product roadmap is tied to revenue, not features. Every build decision answers the question: does this unlock the next enterprise customer or increase retention of the last?
