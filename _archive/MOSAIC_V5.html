The Vision

Physical World Intelligence Infrastructure

Mosaic is not a CPG tool. CPG is the wedge. We are building the platform layer between the physical world and every organization that depends on it.

"GPS told us where things are. Mosaic tells us what they are. That shift — from coordinate to content — is the founding insight of a generational infrastructure company."
The thesis in one sentence.

There are 6.8 billion smartphones on this planet. Each one has a camera, GPS, and more processing power than a 1990s supercomputer. Together they represent the most distributed sensor network ever assembled. And yet the physical world is almost entirely dark.

A brand manager in London is blind to what her products look like on a shelf in Leeds. A CPG director in New York doesn't know that 600 of his stores have been out of stock for eight days. A pricing strategist in Stockholm can't see the competitor promotion that launched yesterday at CVS. An insurer in Frankfurt has zero visibility on property conditions at renewal time. A logistics network in São Paulo is routing blind.

The problem is not technological. The data exists. The sensors exist. The AI to interpret it exists. What has never existed is the platform that organizes human attention into structured, machine-readable truth at a price point and speed that makes physical world intelligence routine.

That is what Mosaic builds. We start in CPG — the highest-frequency, highest-value beachhead — and expand the platform to insurance, real estate, logistics, government, and alternative data. Every vertical. One infrastructure layer.
