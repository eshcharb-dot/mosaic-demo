## The Problem

Brands spend $35B/year trying to understand what's happening inside stores — and still can't answer basic questions like "Is my product in stock right now?" Traditional field audits cost $50–$300 per store visit, take 2–3 weeks to complete, and cover less than 5% of locations. The result: $1.1 trillion in annual out-of-stock losses globally. The physical world generates no data unless someone deliberately captures it.

## The Solution

Mosaic turns any smartphone into a verified intelligence collection device. We match gig workers already traveling past stores to shelf-audit tasks — on their commute, with no detour. AI (GPT-4V class models) converts a photo into structured compliance data in under 30 seconds. A brand manager posts a task in 90 seconds. Results arrive in 4 hours, not 3 weeks.

## Why Mosaic Wins

**Speed**: 4-hour SLA vs. the 2–3 week industry standard.
**Cost**: $8–$25 per task vs. $50–$300 for traditional audits.
**Scale**: Commute Mode — the first gig platform designed to work with your existing daily route, not demand detours. LTV:CAC ratio of 90:1 for commute-mode agents.
**Privacy**: On-device face redaction via MediaPipe/TFLite. GDPR-native from day one — no biometric data ever leaves the device. Competitors can't retrofit this.
**Business model**: Three revenue tiers (Consumer $5–$25/task, SMB $49–$749/mo, Enterprise $2.5K–$50K/mo) plus a 95%-margin Training Data tier that turns every task into a proprietary AI training asset.
