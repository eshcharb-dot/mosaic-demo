Risk Register

Top Risks + Mitigations

⚠️ In-Store Access Without Retailer Consent
Mitigation: Mystery shopper legal framework (50+ year established practice). Build retailer partnership program by Month 6 — signed agreements block competitors and remove legal exposure entirely.
⚠️ BIPA Liability (Illinois Biometric Law)
Mitigation: On-device redaction before upload means no biometric data is ever created or transmitted — this is a technical fact, not a policy claim. Delay Illinois launch until external legal opinion confirms posture.
⚠️ Agent Density Below Fulfillment Threshold
Mitigation: Don't accept customer tasks in any market below 200 active agents. Surge pricing to attract agents when utilization is high. City Captain role to locally manage density. Kill switch: pause tasks at <50% fulfillment rate.
⚠️ Uber or DoorDash Competitive Entry
Mitigation: Win enterprise contracts and build proprietary AI models before a scale player moves. The data moat and retailer access agreements become the defense. Speed is the only real answer.
🔶 AI Accuracy Failure in New Store Formats
Mitigation: Human fallback queue for edge cases. Contractual accuracy guarantees phased in as model matures. Evidently AI for drift detection when launching in new regions.
