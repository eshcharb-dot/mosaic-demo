## One-Paragraph Investment Thesis

Mosaic is building the physical world's intelligence layer — an on-demand crowdsourced platform that turns any smartphone into a verified visual data collection device, accessible to anyone from a solo hot sauce brand owner to a Fortune 500 CPG company. We operate a three-tier marketplace: (1) individual consumers buy one-off tasks via credit card in 90 seconds, (2) SMBs subscribe for recurring brand intelligence starting at $49/month, and (3) enterprise accounts get custom AI models and SLA contracts at $2,500–$50,000+/month. Enterprises post tasks; our agents capture verified visual data; our AI delivers structured insights within hours. We charge $5–$150/task, pay agents 40%, and maintain 55%+ gross margin. The data flywheel — real-world labeled visual data produced as a byproduct — becomes a $2M+/month training data revenue stream at scale. We are 18 months from $9.9M ARR across 12 cities with 10,000 active users and 50 enterprise customers. We are raising $2.5M to prove the three-tier model in 2 cities. The market is $35B and growing at 22% CAGR. The Uber analogy is exact: we are building the consumer layer that enterprise-only competitors have missed.

## The Problem

Businesses and individuals make important decisions based on data that's weeks old, survey-based, and covers less than 10% of locations. The physical world is opaque — and enterprises and consumers both suffer.

**Enterprise pain:**
- CPG brands lose 4–8% of revenue to out-of-stocks they cannot see
- Mystery shopping takes 2–3 weeks and costs $50–$300/visit
- Enterprise field sales teams cover fewer than 5% of store locations
- Real-time competitive pricing data from physical stores does not exist
- Insurance claims require field adjusters who are chronically understaffed

**Consumer and SMB pain (the missed market):**
- A small brand owner with 200 stores has no idea what their shelf looks like
- A consumer driving across town to pick up a baby formula has no verified stock data
- A first-dater has no current photos of the restaurant they booked
- A traveler has no way to verify their Airbnb neighborhood looks as described
- A parent doesn't know if the park is crowded before loading up the car

**The unifying insight**: The physical world generates no data unless someone deliberately captures it. Mosaic makes that capture effortless for anyone — from a CPG VP to someone on the way to the airport who wants to earn $15.

---

## The Solution

Mosaic is a two-sided marketplace for physical world intelligence, now with three customer tiers:

**Supply side**: People download the Mosaic agent app, receive geo-targeted task offers, accept tasks, capture photos/video (with automatic face redaction built in), and earn $2–$30/task.

**Demand side (three tiers)**:
1. **Consumers**: Post a one-off task in 90 seconds, pay by credit card, get AI-extracted results within 24–48 hours
2. **SMBs**: Subscribe for recurring brand intelligence starting at $49/month, self-serve dashboard
3. **Enterprise**: Annual contracts with custom AI models, SLAs, and Salesforce/Snowflake integration

The three pillars that make this possible in 2026:
1. **Ubiquitous smartphones** — 6.8B smartphone users, any of whom can be an agent
2. **On-device AI** — MediaPipe + custom TFLite models enable real-time face redaction and quality validation before upload
3. **Gig economy scale** — 50M+ gig workers globally comfortable earning from micro-tasks

## What Mosaic Is Not

- Not passive surveillance — agents always explicitly accept individual tasks
- Not a background data collection app — zero ambient capture
- Not a facial recognition platform — faces are automatically blurred before any data leaves the device
- Not enterprise-only — any individual can post a task in 90 seconds with a credit card

## Business Model — Three-Tier Architecture

### Tier 1: Consumer / Hobbyist
- **Model**: Credit wallet, pay-per-task, no commitment
- **Price range**: $5–$25/task
- **Examples**: Restaurant scout, crowd check, in-stock verification, neighborhood preview
- **Gross margin**: 52%
- **Acquisition**: App store, social media, viral/referral

### Tier 2: SMB / Self-Serve
- **Model**: Monthly subscription ($49–$749/month)
- **Use cases**: Small brand shelf monitoring, franchise audits, real estate documentation
- **Gross margin**: 62%
- **Acquisition**: Shopify/Faire partnerships, content marketing, PLG

### Tier 3: Enterprise
- **Model**: Annual contracts ($2,500–$50,000+/month)
- **Use cases**: CPG shelf audits, insurance claims, OOH compliance, real estate site selection
- **Gross margin**: 58%
- **Acquisition**: Founder/AE sales, partner channel

### Revenue Streams (All Tiers)
- Per-task pricing (Tiers 1–3)
- Monthly subscriptions (Tiers 2–3)
- AI training data licensing ($0.10–$2.00/labeled image)
- Report products and analytics
- Data intelligence feed (Year 2+, hedge funds + investment research)

**Agent payout**: 40% of task price across all tiers
**Platform blended gross margin**: 55% early-stage → 65%+ at scale

## Competitive Moat

Four layers of defensibility (unchanged) plus one new layer:

1. **Data moat** (strongest): Proprietary labeled real-world visual datasets
2. **Retailer access agreements**: Signed access blocks competitors
3. **Enterprise integrations**: Salesforce, Snowflake, Tableau
4. **Network effects**: More agents → better fulfillment → more customers
5. **Consumer brand identity (new)**: Once Mosaic is "the app you use to check a place before you go," it's a consumer habit that's extremely hard to displace — like Google Maps or Yelp. The consumer brand moat compounds with every viral sharing event.

## Top Risks + Mitigations (Updated)

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| In-store access without retailer consent | High | Mystery shopper framework; retailer partnership program; exterior-first strategy |

| SMB price sensitivity | Medium | Spark tier at $49/month is below most SMB software spend thresholds |
| Two-sided cold start per city | Medium | Enterprise pre-commitment before city launch; consumer tasks create base demand; ambassador seeding |
