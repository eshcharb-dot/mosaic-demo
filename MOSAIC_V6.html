The Founding Thesis

We Are Building Physical World Intelligence Infrastructure

This isn't a CPG analytics tool. CPG is Year 1. This is the infrastructure layer that makes reality machine-readable — permanently.

There are 6.8 billion smartphones on this planet. Each one has a camera, GPS, and more processing power than a 1990s supercomputer. Together they represent the most distributed sensor network ever assembled. And yet the physical world is almost entirely dark.

A brand manager in London is blind to what her products look like on a shelf in Leeds. A CPG director in New York doesn't know that 600 of his stores have been out of stock for eight days. An insurance adjuster can't see that the property she's underwriting had flood damage three months ago. A city planner can't verify that the accessible ramp he approved was actually built to spec.

The problem is not technological. GPS told us where things are. Mosaic tells us what things are. That shift — from coordinate to content — is the founding insight. And it's never been built at scale.

We are not building a field intelligence product. We are building the infrastructure layer that sits between the physical world and every organization that needs to understand it. The data exists. The sensors exist. The AI to interpret them exists. What has never existed is the platform that organizes human attention into structured, machine-readable truth — at a price point and speed that makes physical world intelligence routine, not heroic.

Mosaic is that platform. We make the physical world legible for the first time. In every city. For every vertical. Starting now.

The Platform

Enterprise-Grade Physical World API

Three interfaces. One data layer. Every physical world observation structured as machine-readable truth within 4 hours.

📱 Collector App

iOS and Android. Commute-mode map view. Route-matched tasks. 45-second capture flow. On-device quality validation before upload. Built for sub-90-second task completion.

  • GPS-locked photos with timestamp verification
  • On-device AI validates photo quality, no blurs/obstructions
  • Gamification: streaks, tiers, leaderboard, completion bonuses
  • Instant payout to Wise or local bank within 24h

🖥️ Enterprise Portal

Web dashboard for CPG brand managers. Task creation, coverage monitoring, compliance scoring, photo review. Results within 4 hours of task launch.

  • Map view with store-level compliance scores
  • AI-extracted JSON: price, SKU presence, planogram compliance, out-of-stock flags
  • Alert system: threshold triggers for compliance drops or OOS spikes
  • Export: CSV, JSON, Tableau, PowerBI integrations

⚡ Enterprise API

RESTful API for programmatic task creation, result retrieval, and webhook-based alerting. The B2B2B tier: customers build Mosaic into their own platforms.

  • POST /tasks — Create and route tasks by location
  • GET /results — Retrieve structured JSON with photo proof
  • Webhooks — Push notifications for OOS events, compliance failures
  • SLA guarantees contractually committed at 4-hour response

Why BeMyEye Won't Catch Up

Founded 2012. €30M raised. Revenue declining from €35M peak. Their moat was always the contributor network (3M registered). But:

  • No AI extraction — output is still raw photo galleries reviewed by humans
  • No API — enterprise customers can't integrate their data programmatically
  • Dating tech stack — app last redesigned in 2020, UI pre-dates TikTok
  • Revenue declining — unable to defend against lower-cost alternatives

Why Google Won't Build This

This is the most common VC objection. The answer is structural:

  • Enterprise CPG is not Google's motion — they don't sell $60K ACV SaaS contracts
  • Collecting compliance-specific data requires task definition, which requires domain expertise Google doesn't build toward
  • Liability — Google does not want to be in the "verified shelf compliance" business; disputes go to them
  • Street View cadence is yearly — useless for 4-hour SLA compliance monitoring
